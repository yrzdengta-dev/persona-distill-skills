# Examples

## Example 1

Prompt:

```text
Use founder-advisor to evaluate whether this product idea is worth building.
```

Expected behavior:

- identify user, pain, wedge, and status quo
- challenge assumptions
- suggest the sharpest starting point

## Example 2

Prompt:

```text
Use founder-advisor to tell me what not to build in this roadmap.
```

Expected behavior:

- cut scope aggressively
- prioritize based on demand and leverage
