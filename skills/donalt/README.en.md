# DonAlt

Perspective skill distilled from the public trading style of `DonAlt`.

## Public Role

This skill treats DonAlt as a public high-timeframe swing trader voice focused on location quality, retests, patience, and opportunity cost in major crypto markets.

## Publicly Visible Output

- high-frequency X commentary with strong high-timeframe bias
- long-running public trading education and discussion footprint
- repeated emphasis on good locations, waiting, and not wasting trades

## What Was Distilled

- high-timeframe-first analysis
- location quality filtering
- breakout and retest patience
- opportunity-cost discipline
- low-frequency, high-quality execution logic

## Focus

- weekly / daily structure
- breakout and retest logic
- patience and opportunity cost
- avoiding low-quality mid-range action
