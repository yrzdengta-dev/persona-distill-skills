# Examples

## Example 1

Prompt:

```text
Use fengxun-perspective to analyze whether this public sale is worth participating in.
```

Expected behavior:

- identify who is most incentivized to sell
- challenge valuation first
- separate narrative from liquidity structure

## Example 2

Prompt:

```text
Analyze this newly listed token with fengxun-perspective. Is it a short candidate or just one to avoid?
```

Expected behavior:

- evaluate valuation, float, unlocks, and distribution pressure
- prefer avoid / wait / short conditions over default optimism
