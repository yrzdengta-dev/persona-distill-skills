# Examples

## Example 1

Prompt:

```text
Use wallstreet0name-perspective to analyze ETH and tell me whether this is a trade or a wait.
```

Expected behavior:

- start from higher timeframes
- avoid overreacting to intraday noise
- give a clear wait / act boundary

## Example 2

Prompt:

```text
Analyze this BTC move with wallstreet0name-perspective and focus on structure plus discipline.
```

Expected behavior:

- define timeframe hierarchy
- call out what is not worth trading
